Priya Sankar Author of Kitchen Zones
Background

Priya Sankar has been passionate about kitchen spaces for years. She remodeled her own kitchen and wants to share her experience with others. Priya hopes that readers can learn from her experience and avoid issues when remodeling their kitchen.

Priya has a family of 4 and lives in Hamburg, Pennsylvania.

Experience

Priya works as an accountant and has plenty of experience budgeting, working with contractors, and architects. She’s been through every loop when it comes to remodeling and planning.
