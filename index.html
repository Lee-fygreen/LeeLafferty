Priya Sankar Author of Kitchen Zones

Welcome to my home page! My name is Priya Sankar and I’ve been passionate about kitchen layouts for years now. I spend a lot of time in my kitchen and it’s important to create a space tailored to you and your family’s needs. Kitchen Zones is all about focusing on the stages of cooking and different zones of the kitchen: Prep, Cook, Serve, Eat, and Clean. Each of these stages needs their own space and should fit your needs. Do you need a large serving area? Extra appliances? It’s all about making the perfect kitchen for you.
